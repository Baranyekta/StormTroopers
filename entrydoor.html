ARE ENTRY IMPACT DOORS SAFE?

Entry Impact Doors are crafted to offer a perfect blend of security, durability, and style. Designed to protect against extreme weather, these doors provide unparalleled safety for your home without compromising on aesthetic appeal. With impact-resistant features and advanced locking systems, they are ideal for areas prone to hurricanes and storms.

  • Impact-Resistant Glass: Built to withstand high winds, flying debris, and severe storms, these doors ensure your home remains secure during extreme weather events.
  • Advanced Locking Mechanism: Equipped with multi-point locking systems, Entry Impact Doors offer enhanced security by locking at multiple points along the frame.
  • Reinforced Frame Construction: Made with strong materials like reinforced steel or aluminum, these doors provide an additional layer of security against forced entry.
  • Weatherproof Seals: High-quality seals protect against water and air leakage, ensuring your home stays insulated while enhancing door durability.
  • Shatterproof Glass Technology: The impact-resistant glass is designed to reduce the risk of shattering, ensuring your home is safeguarded against breakage and injury.
  • Customizable Safety Features: Entry Impact Doors can be equipped with various safety options, including reinforced locks or childproof mechanisms.
