ARE FRENCH IMPACT DOORS SAFE?

French Impact Doors are designed to offer a perfect blend of elegance and security, making them a safe and stylish choice for homeowners. With impact-resistant glass and robust security features, they provide unmatched protection, particularly in areas prone to hurricanes and severe weather.

  • Impact-Resistant Glass: Engineered to withstand high winds, flying debris, and severe weather, this specialized glass ensures your home stays protected during storms.
  • Multi-Point Locking Systems: French doors are equipped with advanced locking mechanisms that secure the door at multiple points along the frame for enhanced protection.
  • Reinforced Frames: Constructed from durable materials like reinforced aluminum or steel, these frames add an extra layer of structural strength and security.
  • Weatherproof Seals: High-quality seals and gaskets prevent air and water infiltration while also making the doors more resistant to external tampering.
  • Shatterproof Technology: In addition to impact resistance, the glass is designed to minimize hazardous shards in the event of breakage, enhancing overall safety.
  • Customizable Security Options: French doors can be outfitted with additional safety features, such as keyed locks or childproof latches, to meet specific needs.
