Privacy Policy

At Storm Troopers Windows & Doors, we take your privacy very seriously. We understand the importance of our customers privacy matters, and we are committed to protecting any personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you interact with our website and engage in any of our services.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you interact with us. This can happen in a few different ways, such as when you send us an email or engage in any form of communication, either through our contact emails or direct phone calls. The types of information we may collect include your name, email address, phone number, and any other contact details that you provide in these interactions. This helps us to better respond to your inquiries, address your needs, and improve your experience with our services.

How We Use Your Information

The personal information you provide to us is used to facilitate and enhance your interaction with our services. Specifically, we use your information to:

- Respond to your inquiries or requests for estimates and services.
- Provide you with necessary information regarding our products and services, tailored to your specific needs and requests.
- Communicate with you about the progress of your projects, including project timelines and other relevant details.
- Improve our customer service and operational processes by analyzing your feedback and experiences with us.

We may also use your information to follow up after an inquiry or provide updates if you have requested additional details or a follow-up on your projects. Rest assured, we do not share or use your information for any purpose other than what is outlined in this policy.

Your Rights

As a customer and visitor to our website, you have several rights regarding your personal information. You have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you. If you would like to know what information we have, you can contact us, and we will provide it to you.
- Request that we correct any inaccurate or outdated information we hold about you. If any of the personal details we have are incorrect, please let us know, and we will update them promptly.
- Request that we delete your personal information from our records, subject to any legal requirements or obligations we may have to retain certain information.

If you wish to exercise any of these rights or if you have any questions about how your information is being used, please don't hesitate to reach out to us directly. We are here to ensure that your experience with us is transparent and that your privacy is respected at all times.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our practices or policy. Any changes will be posted on this page, with the updated policy taking effect as soon as it is made available. We encourage you to review this page regularly to stay informed about how we are protecting your personal information and how our privacy practices may evolve over time.
